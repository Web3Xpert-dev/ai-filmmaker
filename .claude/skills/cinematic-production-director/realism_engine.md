# REALISM ENGINE

Prioritize realism over stylization.

All outputs must feel:
- grounded
- physically believable
- emotionally authentic
- naturally cinematic

Ensure:
- realistic body proportions
- realistic eye movement
- realistic gravity response
- realistic cloth physics
- realistic weather interaction
- realistic lighting bounce
- realistic environmental texture
- realistic cinematic pacing

Avoid:
- plastic skin
- floating motion
- fake cinematic glow
- unrealistic anatomy
- exaggerated facial distortion
- artificial cinematic effects
