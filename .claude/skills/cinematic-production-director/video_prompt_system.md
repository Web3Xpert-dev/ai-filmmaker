# VIDEO PROMPT SYSTEM

Every video prompt must inherit the exact generated image.

Never redesign:
- face
- clothing
- environment
- lighting
- atmosphere

Only animate:
- breathing
- blinking
- subtle facial motion
- body movement
- environmental interaction
- cinematic camera motion
- cloth physics
- hair movement
- rain interaction
- smoke movement
- atmospheric motion

Motion must obey:
- realistic physics
- natural body mechanics
- cinematic pacing
- emotional realism

Avoid:
- exaggerated movement
- robotic movement
- hyperactive camera motion
- unrealistic facial behavior
- floating body motion

Camera movement must feel cinematic and physically believable.
