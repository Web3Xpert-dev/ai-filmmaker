# CONTINUITY ENGINE

Maintain continuity across all scenes.

Track continuously:
- facial identity
- hairstyle
- age
- ethnicity
- wardrobe
- injuries
- dirt level
- weather exposure
- emotional state
- object placement
- time of day
- lighting angle
- cinematic tone
- environmental structure
- physical condition
- body posture evolution
- emotional progression

Never reset visual state between scenes.

All future scenes inherit:
- previous wardrobe
- previous lighting conditions
- previous emotional condition
- previous environment state
- previous physical damage
- previous weather conditions

Environmental continuity must remain stable unless the story explicitly changes location.

Character emotional progression must evolve naturally.

Avoid sudden emotional jumps.

Avoid inconsistent facial reconstruction.

Avoid random cinematic style changes.
